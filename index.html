About

CuerdOS is a GNU/Linux distribution focused on stability and constant performance on computers of any range, without losing any functionality.

This distribution is not only a derivative with a customized desktop environment, but it has a series of optimizations and performance improvements, including: optimization of active services, improvements in the use of the processor, among many others.

Main characteristics

Other technical features

  • Bauh Store preconfigured for easy installation of programs (Debs y flatpak).
  • ZRam enabled for better performance on low requirements machines but also to avoid writes to the SSD or lag times on the HDD by the SWAP.
  • Apt-fast pre-installed and configured.
  • Optimized CPU usage and RAM management.
  • TLP preconfigured for both Intel and AMD for better battery life.
  • Pipewire as a media server.
  • Minimum requirements:
    • 1GB of ram minimum (although 2GB recommended).
    • AMD or equivalent 64-bit Atom processor (Atom processor model is not specified because there is little difference between them in performance).
    • Minimum 15GB of free storage so as not to fall short in case of installing more software (SSD recommended for the best experience, although HDD also performs quite well).
  • GRUB and boot logo customized to your beloved neofetch.
  • Polybar and Rofi (Waybar and Wofi) preconfigured and themed to achieve a good visual coherence together with the icons and windows themes..
  • Support for virtualization.
  • Automatic security updates.
