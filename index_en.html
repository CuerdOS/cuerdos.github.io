About

CuerdOS is a GNU/Linux distribution focused on stability and constant performance from low to high resources, without losing functionality.

This distribution is not just a derivative with a custom desktop environment, but it has a series of optimizations and performance improvements, among which are: The use of its own kernel, optimization of active services, improvements in clock speed processor, among many others.
